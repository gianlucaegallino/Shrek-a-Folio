About Me

Hello there! I’m Shrek, a big, green, somewhat grumpy ogre who loves his privacy and a bit of swampy peace and quiet. I’m not one for fancy words or crowds, but I do appreciate good food, a warm fire, and the occasional adventure—though I'd rather keep things simple. If you’re looking for someone to save the day or take down a dragon (or two), well, I’m your ogre.

My Friends
